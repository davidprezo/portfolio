WALK CYCLE

Motion Graphics

Good animation is a big part in how a motion graphics composition can be hugely effective. Walk cycles are one of the hardest animations to nail down, so this quirky and colourful video made in After Effects took some time to put together.

This was the sketch of the character I made prior to animating. I then made the character in illustrator and used the puppet tool to make the character walk.
